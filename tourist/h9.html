About Fatehpur Sikri

Fatehpur Sikri, located in Uttar Pradesh, is a historical city and former capital of the Mughal Empire. The city is known for its stunning Mughal architecture, and one of its most famous landmarks is the Buland Darwaza (Gate of Victory), which is a massive entryway to the Jama Masjid.

This city was declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site due to its rich history and architectural significance.
