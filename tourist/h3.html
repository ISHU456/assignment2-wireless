About Agra Fort

Agra Fort, located in the city of Agra, Uttar Pradesh, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The fort was originally built by the Mughal Emperor Akbar in the 16th century.

The fort contains various structures like the Jahangiri Mahal, the Diwan-i-Khas, and the Diwan-i-Aam, all of which have intricate Mughal architecture and decoration.
