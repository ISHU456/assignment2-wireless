About Lakshmana Temple

The Lakshmana Temple, located in Khajuraho, Madhya Pradesh, is a stunning example of the Chandela dynasty's architectural brilliance. Dedicated to Lord Vishnu, it is known for its intricate sculptures and grand design, making it one of the most beautiful temples in India.

This temple is part of the UNESCO World Heritage Site group of temples in Khajuraho, which are renowned for their detailed carvings and unique architectural style.
