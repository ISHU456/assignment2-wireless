About Ellora Caves

Ellora Caves are a complex of rock-cut temples and monasteries located in Maharashtra. They date back to between the 5th and 10th centuries and contain 34 caves that serve as Buddhist, Hindu, and Jain temples.

The most famous structure is the Kailasa Temple, an elaborate single monolithic rock excavation that is a remarkable feat of engineering and architecture.
