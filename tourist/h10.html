About Pattadakal

Pattadakal, located in Karnataka, is known for its stunning temples and rich history. It was once the capital of the Chalukya dynasty and is now a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The site is famous for its unique architecture that combines both North and South Indian temple styles.

The temples at Pattadakal are known for their intricate carvings and are a significant part of India's cultural heritage.
