About Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, is one of the most iconic landmarks of India. It was built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, who passed away during childbirth in 1631.

Known for its stunning white marble architecture and its majestic gardens, the Taj Mahal is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and attracts millions of visitors every year.
