About Ajanta Caves

The Ajanta Caves are a group of 30 rock-cut Buddhist cave monuments located in the state of Maharashtra. These caves contain exquisite paintings and sculptures that date back to the 2nd century BCE to about 480 CE.

The Ajanta Caves are known for their beautiful murals and architecture, which depict the life of Lord Buddha and various religious themes. They are a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
