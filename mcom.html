Mcom

Course Overview

The MCom at College Institute of Engineering and Technology (DAVV) is designed to provide students with an in-depth understanding of English literature, language, and culture. Students will explore various literary genres, historical contexts, and critical theories.

Course Structure

The course includes a combination of core and elective subjects, designed to enhance both academic and writing skills. Students will also have opportunities for internships and projects related to language and literature.

Admission Requirements

Applicants must have completed their higher secondary education (12th grade) with a minimum of 50% marks, with English as a core subject. Entrance exams and personal interviews may be required for selection.
